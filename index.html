Water Leak Stop



The WLS-1, is a system for detection and management of water leaks. In such a case it will close the water supply and notify you immediately.

   

The WLS-1 accepts moisture/water sensors for water leak detection, flow sensors for monitoring the consumption of time, electric valve for the main water supply management and contacts to interface with any existing home alarm system and further processing of information.

The WLS-1 is able to find out and signal leak from:

  • A water sensor that is placed at a point where a leak is very likely to happen. Such is the space under the sink, water heater, the floor of a computer room and where it is needed.
  • A flow sensor at the entry point of the main water supply, which monitoring the continuous flow time. In excess of the defined threshold an alarm is triggered. For exceptional cases, e.g. filling a pool, a bypass function for one cycle is available.

Control your WLS through a GSM communicator (not included):

  • Remote control of the main water valve status.
  • Reset to detection mode after an alarm.

Optionally, the WLS-1 can be programmed not to close the mains valve, but only give notice, in a case of an alarm.

Benefits:

  • Dual supervision:
    • Î’y monitoring the maximum allowed time, of continuous flow.
    • By monitoring sensitive areas through humidity sensors of various types (Pin, cable, etc.).
  • Single supervision: Versatile because it can detect leaks by using only humidity sensors or the maximum allowed flow time, through the flow sensor.
  • Control the main water supply via electromagnetic valve.
  • Bypass function to manage longer periods of deliberate continuous water flow, such as filling a pool without changing the main program.
  • Integration with an existing alarm system, giving the following advantages:
    • Perform operations such as the transmission of the alarm.
    • Remote control of the electromagnetic valve.

Additional features supported:

  • Connect an indoor or an outdoor siren.
  • Connect a PSTN communicator (only notification) or a GSM communicator (remote control funtion).
  • Connect an external wireless system that will transfer the information of the input- output signals. For example using a transmitter compatible with the alarm system of the house, you can transmit a leak event to it, without cables.
  • The use of WDT (watchdog timer) and special firmware, ensures the proper functioning of the device.

Power Supply:

Voltage: 12V DC

Current (monitoring / max): 25 / 155mA

Dimensions: 106 x 86 x 58 mm (L x W x H)

Weight: 180 grams

Features:

Start flow detection (lt/min): According to the sensor's sensitivity.

Flow sensors: Flow switch (NC), Flow sensor (Hall Effect type 12VDC).

Water leak sensors: Points detection probe, sensor cable.

Flow management: A form C relay, manage a solenoid or electrical water valve.

Remote control for (possibility): Reset, Open/Close solenoid water valve, exclude electric valve.

Notification: Internal buzzer, form C relay to trigger an external siren, GSM module, house alarm etc.

MCFT table: 8 different options (5, 10, 20, 30, 45, 60, 90, 150).

Modes: Normal, Bypass 1 Cycle, Exclude Valve (only notification).

Functional Stability: Watchdog timer, Supervised EEPROM
