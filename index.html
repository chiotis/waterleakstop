Water Leak Stop

WLS, is a system which controls and manages water leaks. In this case, it will perform the necessary actions to stop the leak and will inform you immediately.

   

WLS, features water sensors to detect water leak, flow sensor for tracking time consumption, electric valve for managing the main water supply and interface with the home alarm system, for further information processing.

The WLS is able to find out and signal leak from:

  • a water sensor, which is placed at a point where a leak is very likely to occur. Such is the space under the heater, the sink, on the boilerâ€™s room floor, pump room of a swimming pool or wherever necessary. Furthermore the smooth operation of the water sensors is continuously monitored with, appropriate notice in the event of a short circuit line.
  • a flow sensor at the entry point of the main water supply, monitoring the maximum continuous flow time. In excess of the defined threshold an alarm is triggered. For exceptional cases, e.g. filling a pool, a bypass function for one cycle is available.

Control your WLS through a GSM communicator (not included):

  • remote control of the main water valve status.
  • return to normal operation after detecting a leak.

Optionally, WLS can skip closing the water supply during an alarm and send only an alarm notification.

   

   

Benefits:

  • Dual supervision:
    • Based on the excess of the maximum allowed time of continuous water flow.
    • Based on leak detection in sensitive areas, using water sensors.
  • Single supervision: Versatile because it can detect leaks by using only water sensors or calculating the maximum flow time, through the flow sensor.
  • Control the main water supply via electromagnetic valve.
  • Bypass function to manage longer periods of deliberate continuous water flow, such as filling a pool without changing the main program.
  • Integration with an existing alarm system, giving the following advantages:
    • Perform operations such as the transmission of the alarm.
    • Remote control of the electromagnetic valve.

Additional features supported:

  • Connect an indoor or an outdoor siren.
  • Connect a PSTN communicator (only notification) or a GSM communicator (with the ability to remote control WLS).
  • Connect a third party wireless system that will transfer the information of the input- output signals. For example using a transmitter compatible with the alarm system of the house, you can transmit an leak event to it, without cables.
       

Power Supply:

Input voltage: 12V DC

Current (normal / max): 25 / 240mA

Features:

Start flow detection (lt/min): According to the sensor's sensitivity.

Flow sensors: Flow switch (NC), Flow sensor (Hall Effect type 5VDC).

Water leak sensors: Points detection probe.

Flow management: A form C relay, manage a solenoid water valve.

Notification: Internal buzzer, form C relay to trigger an external siren, GSM module, house alarm etc.

Remote control for: Reset, Open/Close solenoid water valve.

Trigger times: 8 different options.

Modes: Normal, Bypass 1 Cycle, Exclude Valve.
